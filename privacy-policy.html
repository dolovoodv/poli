Your data, handled with care.

Information we collect

We only collect data required to provide car rental services and meet legal or contractual obligations.

  • Identification details such as name, phone, email, and driving license data.
  • Rental details including booking dates, vehicle selection, and payment confirmation.
  • Communication records when you request support or ask for a quote.

How we use your data

Data is used to deliver rentals, handle service requests, and comply with applicable regulations.

  • Prepare rental contracts and verify eligibility.
  • Coordinate vehicle pickup, return, and support.
  • Maintain records required by legal or accounting rules.

Sharing and retention

We do not sell your data. It may be shared only with trusted partners when required for service delivery or legal compliance.

Retention

Data is kept only as long as necessary for rentals, legal requirements, and service history.

Third parties

We share data with payment or insurance partners only when required to complete a rental.

Security

We apply reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect stored data.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data, subject to legal limits.

  • Request a copy of your personal data on file.
  • Ask for corrections if information is inaccurate.
  • Request deletion when data is no longer required.
